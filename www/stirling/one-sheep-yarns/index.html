One-Sheep Yarns

One-Sheep Yarns are just that: yarns that can be traced back to a single sheep that has a name and a history. We get our fleeces from farmers that treat their sheep very well, and always have a story to tell about each one. Each fleece is milled (by small, family run mills in the US) into yarn that best showcases the wool. One sheep can produce between 1-4 sweaters worth of yarn each year!
